



# What's next

Those of us who like to ponder the cosmos know of an idea called The Great Filter: that there is some mechanism out there that prevents civilizations from advancing to a point of sufficient technology to proliferate through their galaxy.
Should such a filter exist, I can find no more fitting one than the following filter we seem to put on ourselves. 

Many of us spend some segment of our youth aware that the world as it is kind of sucks.
Eventually this line of reasoning runs into others with the same awareness, and you have to face the fact that simply pointing out the world sucks is neither that novel nor that interesting. 
You may then have the audacity to believe you might be able to do something about it, which leads you to the realization that since you haven't, the fact the world sucks is partly your fault.

So whatcha gonna do?
Win over the hearts and minds of eight billion people, or fire up the vape and the X Box, and forget you ever had a chance?
